About Me

I am an IT undergraduate at Camarines Norte State College with hands-on experience building web, mobile, and embedded solutions. I develop responsive websites and web apps using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and create native Android applications with Kotlin and Java. On the backend I work with databases (MySQL) and APIs to deliver reliable, data-driven features. I also enjoy game development and 3D rendering, and have practical experience using C++ and game frameworks for small projects. In the IoT space I’ve integrated microcontrollers and sensors to create connected prototypes and telemetry solutions. I focus on writing clean, maintainable code, designing intuitive user interfaces, and iterating quickly to solve real problems. I’m currently seeking opportunities to collaborate on projects or internships where I can contribute my skills and continue learning modern development practices.

Projects

E-LAURA: Elektronikong Laro at Ugnayan sa Florante at Laura

E-LAURA is an interactive quiz-based game inspired by Florante at Laura. It challenges players with engaging questions about the classic Filipino literary work, enhancing their knowledge while making learning fun and interactive.

Farm Villa Private Resort Booking System

Farm Villa Private Resort Booking System is a user-friendly platform designed to streamline resort reservations. It allows guests to browse available accommodations, check pricing, and book their stay with ease.

INKI: Automated Printing Kiosk

A self-service printing solution designed for convenience and efficiency. It allows users to print documents, photos, and other materials instantly without the need for staff assistance.

San Vicente Legislative Tracking System

San Vicente Legislative Tracking System is a digital platform designed to streamline the management and monitoring of legislative documents, ordinances, and resolutions. It improves transparency and governance by tracking status and history.

DogGo: Veterinary Clinic Management System With IoT Collar Tracking Device

DogGo is a veterinary clinic management system with IoT collar tracking and RFID-based information to help monitor and manage pets effectively.

Until Sunrise Survival Game

Until Sunrise is a survival game where players must survive the night and find a way to escape the island before sunrise. The game won Best Visuals and Best UI/UX at the IoT Expo 2025 (theme: "Designing Tomorrow: Interactive, Connected, and Immersive Technologies") on December 15, 2025.
